How Ionvale Works

Empowering

Ionvale supports disadvantaged designers by providing the vintage materials and machinery needed to produce their one of a kind pieces. We sell their items on our e-commerce platform, giving them the majority of profits earned.

Environmental

You will have peace of mind knowing all of our materials used are being repurposed from vintage materials. They come from trusted and vetted supply chains. This is part of our mission to minimise our carbon footprint.
